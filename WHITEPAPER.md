## 4. Consensus

SHA-256d Proof of Work.
Same algorithm as Bitcoin.
64-bit nonce space.
Difficulty adjusts every 2016 blocks.
Target block time: 5 minutes.

---

## 5. Wallet and Transactions

secp256k1 elliptic curve cryptography.
Schnorr signatures for efficiency.
BIP-39 recovery phrases (in development).
UTXO model like Bitcoin.

---

## 6. Networking

Custom P2P protocol.
Port 8633.
Nodes connect and share blocks automatically.
Anyone can run a node.

---

## 7. Lightning Network

Native HTLC payment channels.
Instant transactions off-chain.
Settles to main chain.

---

## 8. DAO Governance

On-chain voting built in from day one.
Square-root weighted voting — whales cannot dominate.
5% treasury funded automatically every block.
Community decides how treasury is spent.

---

## 9. Security

- secp256k1 signatures
- Time warp protection (in development)
- DDoS protection (in development)
- Constant time voting (in development)
